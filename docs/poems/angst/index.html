Old Angst

Today was the last day of my life

The last day I prove myself through pain and strife

I expected more than ignorance and hate

But you can’t see logic as the soul form of debate

You criticize my eyes, and how I choose to believe

Yet throw the dirt on everyone’s ability to perceive

As if you were made perfect, your struggles more triumphant,

As the times you abuse us becomes more and more redundant

You mask yourself with empathy, forgiveness

But below the surface your full of shit and selfishness

So good luck with life, I’ll leave you with that

This is my last day to have your back.

…

My death will only bring tears to the ocean of tragedy in the world
