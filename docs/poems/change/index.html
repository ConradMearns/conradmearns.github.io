Change

Two pills
Pop them
Crawl in
Warm bed
Empty head
Dreams of dread?
They’re all dead

But I’m not

Done growing
I keep showing
Myself I can change
Thoughts rearrange
Sometimes derange
Derail, a torn sail
Cannot bail
From this ship
I’m the captain
And the arsonist
Who started this
Fantastic mess
You thought I would accomplish less?
That low bar is a personal best
That I keep setting
Lower
And lower
Thought I’d be the limbo king by now
But all I wear are khakis and a plastic crown
Fishhooks in my cheeks to prevent a frown
From showing
Not done growing
I still change
I can’t be the same
As my old self
And my health
Neither rises
Or lowers
These lures
Keep catching me
Still got the hooks as you can see
Getting pulled in every which way direction
Cant form a proper sentence
Or ask a simple question
Most of the time
Yet I can still bust rhyme
It’s my way to pass the time
And I seem to do it fairly fine
Which is nice cuz it don’t cost me a dime
But it cost me my life
Working on things I think are important
Create code to capture meaning then store it
Stashed away from any who’d whore it
Out, to those who would misuse it
Rip the meaning right out of it’s socket
Like isolated islands bought by cruise ships
Old songs being turned into shitty pop hits
Knockoff brands for the poor misfits
Misinformation for the dumb and half wits
