Fig Newtons

I got so so high,
I could hear the conversations of many
I felt the happiness
the love
the pain
All the days wasted
Because of rain
Where people’s problems
Evaporate before the drain
I listened to music, that made me think of you
I was alone, only wishing for you
But the music
But my dreams
Kept pulling me
In
To home
To you
You

You
Are the drum
Before the March
The rigor, the real
The sensations
I thought I would never
Never see

Kyle speaks louder
He needs attention
I want to help him
To learn and teach me
How to to touch

Hamgboard time

Broken
Charm
Caulk
Happiness
Yet a tiny
Spark
