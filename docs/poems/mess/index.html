Mess

the problem with loving someone
and leaving your heart with them
is that when you start to feel depressed
you instantly want to be dead
you cant hold on
to your dreams anymore
and you dont care about rhyming

your voice doesnt speak
your actions dont sing
sleep becomes irrelevant
and your ears sometimes ring

but you still want to tell people
exactly how you feel
you just dont have the names of anyone
that you think might want to hear

you cry at random times

i cry at random times
i hate to talk about my problems
i dont know why
i cant capitalize my i’s
or prioritize my life

my head is a mess
my head is a mess
i hate being like this
my head is a mess
