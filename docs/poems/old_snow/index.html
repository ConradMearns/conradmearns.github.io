Old Snow

It’s cold today
But that’s okay
Because even though the leaves and I are falling
This autumn weather is still soft and charming
As time moves forward it does less and less harming
And things move towards harmony

I remember the old days
Those November/December cold days
When we’d walk and shiver in the winter haze
Before we ever used a word like bae

The nostalgia came and hit me hard
Gave me the energy to write these bars
And the memories of junk food dates in my car
It’s heartbreaking that we’ve become so far

There are still traces of you wherever I go
Emotions captured by falling leaves and snow
But now that we can open up to talk
I can finally let the grieving go
As long you are doing okay, and that I know
