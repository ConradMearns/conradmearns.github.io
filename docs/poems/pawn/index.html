Pawn

Now that I am free of that world
I am alone in a new one
Everything is a copy of what it once was
And I am merely a pawn
But a pawn with confidence
Is a pawn that understands death
Not as a tragedy, but as a choice
The power to make one sacrifice
So everyone else can do their best
