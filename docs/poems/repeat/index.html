Repeat

Don’t drink so much coffee
Before you try and sleep
You’re mind will race with thoughts
Stuck on repeat
Stuck on repeat
Stuck on repeat
And you won’t be scared
But you’ll jerk awake
Making an audible gasp for air
Stuck on repeat
On repeat
On repeat
Thoughts race faster
Like a river during downpour
Yet they go nowhere
Stuck
This is is why you
Repeat
