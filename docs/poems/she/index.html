She

she endless brown eyes
you can peer into forever
a soul thats old and wise
love that only gets better

she has a smile made of sunshine
and hands that know how to work
her kiss and touch is so divine
you’ll know she loves you by her smirk

we talk about our future kids
even though she wont remember
we’ll talk until her clock says six
I could talk to her forver

she’s the most “herself”
i’ve seen a person be
and i couldn’t imagine anyone else
whose love i’d rather receive

distance is doing us a number
but together we are strong
my head is getting number
but hopefully not for long

I’ve started taking medicine
to take away some pain
so that I may be my better self
and this commitment, we can sustain

Tonight,
the sky
looked like
your face
