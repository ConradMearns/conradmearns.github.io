Starlings

I smile at the woman that happens to pass me by
her head drops suddenly
as if my glare was a guillotine
or if I was made of something putrid
I cannot blame her
what if I was?
something putrid

The images dance in my head
with the grace of starlings they move
so fast I cannot catch
what they did with that bat
a starling mimics her voice over the phone
blood
blood
blood
she doesn’t remember me
her starlings have become a hurricane
ripping her mind apart
while mine mimic the pain
to the starling: “i have enough of my own pain, please not now”
the starlings stare at me
even the thought of trying to empty my head
brings the starlings, brings the dread
we think the drug was ketamine
after 3 months, it’s to tell if you know what i mean
though the holes in her arms were still a bit aparant
she was too afraid at the time to get a rape kit
they knew where she lived
they visited her there
but all she recalled were nightmares
but they matched the stains
they matched the pain
her reality was being torn apart
it made her insane
we’d call every night
from 5 to 2, she would only be herself
for a few minutes
maybe a hour
I let my life fall apart
because i loved her

she escaped
and I checked in
to the loony bin
my brain has always been broken
so now when im scared i think of the faceless
what they did what that bat
what I want to do right right back
my dreams have been taken over
my trust has been destroyed
my anxiety is catalyst for the rage they’ve deployed

but i hear he’s in another country
and she doesn’t want to go back
to meet with the school and authorities per invite
i cant fucking blame her
