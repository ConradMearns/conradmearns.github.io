3am - Why

Don’t ask why
Neurons fry
Recursive destruction
Because we yearn to learn why

Pains meds to stop the pain
Alcohol to think less
The more you drink, the more pain you feel
And you can only think about stress

Another mistake
Another self inflicted wound
The RN asks about scars
You wonder if they have them too
