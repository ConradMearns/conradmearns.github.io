Awake

March 04 - 12:19am
I’ve abandoned my therapist. I’m done with people acting like they know this. I see real pain. I see real agony. I see fake stares of concern and I hear fake stories.
I hear concern for yourself, not me. You aren’t suffering, so you wont be worried.

I’ve done this before, I must say. But never with the intent of opening the spillage of neurons behind this thick skull to the world. It’s not possible to keep my pain private when it can’t be hid. So I’ve created this task for myself to hid on the web. I must invest with the kind of love I had when I was a kid. Not an exercise of tasks through my time to be bid.

I wish I were a kid.

Remove the sunset.
It’s sweet flavor only wretches my throat.
But now I can’t shake the need to cry
My eyes are determined to stay dry.

Where did my love go.
